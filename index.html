About

Andrew Trotter is a composer and pianist from Georgia. At seventeen, his catalog spans orchestral, chamber, and solo piano work — including the symphonic poem Tempesta dell'Anima, the four-act ballet The Silver Swan, and the sacred orchestral collection All For My Lord.

Twelve years of classical piano training ground his composing. He has earned first place at the Georgia Music Federation and second place in Piano Ensemble at Bob Jones University.

Andrew writes from a place of faith, and performs across the Atlanta area for sacred services, recitals, and private events.

Currently: finishing high school and preparing for conservatory auditions.
