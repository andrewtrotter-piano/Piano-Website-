About

A young composer at the desk

Andrew Trotter is a seventeen-year-old composer and pianist whose catalog reaches across orchestral, chamber, and solo piano forms — symphony, symphonic poem, ballet, sacred collection, and quieter writing for piano alone.

His work has been recognized in regional competitions, including a first-place finish at the Georgia Music Festival and a second place at the Bob Jones University piano competition. He continues to study, perform, and release new work independently.

His music sits in the slower-listening tradition — written to be sat with, not skimmed — drawing on the line that runs from the European masters through the cinematic composers of today.

Currently finishing high school and preparing the next chapter of his musical training.
